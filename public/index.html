What is Inuarashi Inu?

Inuarashi is an incredibly large and grizzled canine mink. He wears a small pair of blue sunglasses and has shoulder-length black ears, a white beard, and a large and bushy tail. During the battle against Jack, he wore a dark-colored striped suit with a frilled collar, a belt, a light-colored cape, and a helmet. Like all the retainers of the Kozuki Family, Inuarashi bears the family crest somewhere on his body.

Sulong is a bodily transformation that members of the Mink Tribe can undergo temporarily by looking at the full moon.Now, with the sulong state, Inuarashi is here to lead his mink tribe how to make a token moon.
